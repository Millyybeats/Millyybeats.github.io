About Me

Millyy is a music producer from Mount Vernon, New York, known for combining classical music training with modern production. His work has earned over 300,000 views on YouTube, helping him gain recognition for his unique sound and versatility. He studied at the prestigious Juilliard School Pre-College Program for four years, where he developed skills in composition, performance, and music theory. Millyy's style blends hard-hitting drums with atmospheric sounds, inspired by drill, trap, and experimental electronic music. Along with producing for artists, he also creates original drum kits and sample packs for producers online. He is currently in his final year at Berklee College of Music, studying Electronic Production and Design.

Sound Design

League of Legends

Sound design project reimagining a League of Legends trailer that features custom Foley and sound effects. The audio was mixed in 5.1 surround sound to create a more immersive cinematic experience, enhancing spatial depth, environmental detail, and the intensity of in game action.

Motorola Ad

Sound design project featuring a Motorola ad, showcasing original scoring to picture alongside custom sound design. The work focuses on synchronizing music and sound effects to enhance brand storytelling, rhythm, and visual impact. Mixed in 5.1 surround sound to create a dynamic and immersive listening experience.

The Matrix Foley

Foley project featuring custom recorded and mixed sound design over a scene from The Matrix. This piece highlights hands on Foley recording techniques and detailed post-production mixing, to enhance realism, weight, and impact. Mixed in 5.1 surround sound to create a fully immersive and spatially detailed cinematic experience.
