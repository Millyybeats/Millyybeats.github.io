About Me

Millyy is a music producer from Mount Vernon, New York, known for combining classical music training with modern production. His work has earned over 300,000 views on YouTube, helping him gain recognition for his unique sound and versatility. He studied at the prestigious Juilliard School Pre-College Program for four years, where he developed skills in composition, performance, and music theory. Millyy’s style blends hard-hitting drums with atmospheric sounds, inspired by drill, trap, and experimental electronic music. Along with producing for artists, he also creates original drum kits and sample packs for producers online.
